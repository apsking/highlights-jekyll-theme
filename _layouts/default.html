    Where do I go?

    Tucked away in the Oregon's Williamette valley, we are getting married in the tree-lined woodlands at CedarVale! When we first arrived at this venue, we immediately felt relaxed in its towering cedar trees and beautiful scenery. We are excited to welcome you to the Pacific Northwest and share this wonderful space with you as we celebrate our union!

    Airport

    Famous for its iconic carpet, the nearest airport is Portland International Airport (PDX). As Oregon's biggest airport, it should have plenty of options for you to choose from to fly in.
